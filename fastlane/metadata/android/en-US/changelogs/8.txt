Initial release.

- Read and write coin balances on Mifare Classic and NTAG NFC cards
- Dynamic per-card key derivation (HMAC-SHA1)
- Transaction history stored on card (up to 4 entries)
- Card format, top-up, deduct, and reset operations
- Single-recharge mode and age verification support
- Portrait and landscape layouts
- Theme colour presets and custom HSV colour picker
- Decimal mode, audio feedback, vibration, and background flash
